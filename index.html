ABOUT US

Our story is rooted in the ancient traditions of West Africa. Each treatment is specifically designed to reconnect your unique needs — whether you seek relief from stress, recovery from injury, or simply a moment of profound rest.

Our therapies combine the healing power of pure Shea butter with modern techniques to nourish both your body and soul. Every visit to Hamamat is a journey into wellness, from the air you breathe to the skin you're in.
